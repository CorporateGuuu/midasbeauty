Product Details

Our Repair Shampoo is a professional-grade haircare product designed to restore and revitalize damaged hair. Formulated with our exclusive bond-building complex, this shampoo works at the molecular level to repair broken bonds in the hair structure, resulting in stronger, healthier-looking hair.

The sulfate-free formula gently cleanses without stripping color or natural oils, making it ideal for color-treated, chemically processed, heat-styled, or naturally dry hair. Regular use helps prevent breakage, split ends, and frizz while protecting against future damage from heat styling and environmental stressors.

Each bottle contains 250ml (8.4 fl oz) of product and features a convenient pump dispenser for easy application. The formula is pH-balanced and free from parabens, sulfates, and silicones, making it suitable for all hair types, including sensitive scalps.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Sodium Lauroamphoacetate, Sodium Methyl Cocoyl Taurate, Sodium Chloride, Hydrolyzed Keratin, Argania Spinosa Kernel Oil, Panthenol, Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride, Polyquaternium-7, Glycerin, Citric Acid, Tetrasodium EDTA, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Fragrance.

Key Ingredients:

  • Hydrolyzed Keratin: A protein that helps repair damaged hair by filling in gaps in the hair cuticle, strengthening the hair shaft, and improving elasticity.
  • Argan Oil (Argania Spinosa Kernel Oil): Rich in antioxidants, essential fatty acids, and vitamin E, argan oil helps nourish, moisturize, and protect hair from damage.
  • Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5): Penetrates the hair shaft to moisturize from within, adding volume and shine while reducing frizz and split ends.
  • Polyquaternium-7: A conditioning agent that helps detangle hair, reduce static, and improve manageability without weighing hair down.
  • Glycerin: A humectant that attracts and retains moisture in the hair, helping to prevent dryness and brittleness.

How to Use

  1. Thoroughly wet hair with warm water.
  2. Dispense a quarter-sized amount of shampoo into your palm (use more for longer or thicker hair).
  3. Rub palms together to distribute the product.
  4. Apply to scalp and massage gently in circular motions to create a light lather.
  5. Work the shampoo down through the lengths of your hair.
  6. Leave on for 1-2 minutes to allow the repairing ingredients to penetrate the hair shaft.
  7. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  8. For best results, follow with Hydrating Conditioner.

Recommended use: 2-3 times per week, alternating with a gentle cleansing shampoo if needed.

Pro tip: For extremely damaged hair, use as a treatment by applying to dry hair, covering with a shower cap, and leaving on for 15-20 minutes before rinsing. Follow with conditioner for maximum repair benefits.
