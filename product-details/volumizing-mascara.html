Product Details

Our Volumizing Mascara is designed to transform your lashes with dramatic volume and length without clumping. The innovative hourglass-shaped brush captures and coats each lash from root to tip, while the buildable formula allows you to customize your look from natural to bold.

The smudge-proof, flake-resistant formula stays put all day, while lash-conditioning ingredients like biotin and keratin strengthen and nourish your lashes over time. This mascara is ophthalmologist-tested and suitable for sensitive eyes and contact lens wearers.

Each tube contains 10ml (0.33 fl oz) of product and features a precision brush designed to separate and define lashes while adding maximum volume. The formula is water-resistant but not waterproof, making it easy to remove at the end of the day.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Beeswax, Copernicia Cerifera (Carnauba) Wax, Stearic Acid, Glyceryl Stearate, Polybutene, Silica, Panthenol, Biotin, Hydrolyzed Keratin, Tocopheryl Acetate, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Hexylene Glycol, Iron Oxides (CI 77499).

Key Ingredients:

  • Biotin: A B-vitamin that helps strengthen lashes and promotes growth.
  • Hydrolyzed Keratin: Helps repair damaged lashes and improves elasticity.
  • Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5): Conditions and moisturizes lashes, preventing brittleness.
  • Vitamin E (Tocopheryl Acetate): Protects lashes from environmental damage and nourishes the lash line.
  • Beeswax and Carnauba Wax: Natural waxes that help thicken lashes and provide long-lasting wear.

How to Use

  1. Curl lashes if desired before applying mascara.
  2. Position the brush at the base of your upper lashes.
  3. Wiggle the brush from side to side at the base to deposit more product.
  4. Sweep the brush upward from root to tip.
  5. Allow the first coat to dry slightly (about 30 seconds) before applying additional coats.
  6. For lower lashes, hold the brush vertically and gently coat lashes using the tip of the brush.
  7. To remove, use a gentle eye makeup remover or micellar water.

Pro tip: For maximum volume, apply a second or third coat, focusing on the outer lashes for a cat-eye effect.

To prevent clumping: Wipe excess product from the brush on a clean tissue before application, and don't pump the wand in and out of the tube (this introduces air and dries out the formula).
