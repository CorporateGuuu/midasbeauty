Product Details

Our Radiance Serum is a powerful skincare treatment designed to brighten, hydrate, and rejuvenate your skin. Formulated with 20% Vitamin C, this serum helps to fade dark spots, even skin tone, and protect against environmental damage.

The addition of hyaluronic acid provides deep hydration, plumping the skin and reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. This lightweight, fast-absorbing formula is suitable for all skin types and can be used both morning and night.

Each bottle contains 30ml (1 fl oz) of product and is packaged in a dark glass bottle with a dropper applicator to protect the formula from light and air exposure, ensuring maximum potency.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C), Sodium Hyaluronate (Hyaluronic Acid), Ferulic Acid, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Glycerin, Propylene Glycol, Panthenol, Sodium Hydroxide, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin.

Key Ingredients:

  • Vitamin C (20%): A powerful antioxidant that brightens skin, boosts collagen production, and protects against environmental damage.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Attracts and retains moisture, providing deep hydration and plumping the skin.
  • Ferulic Acid: Enhances the stability and effectiveness of Vitamin C and E, providing additional antioxidant protection.
  • Vitamin E: Nourishes and protects the skin, working synergistically with Vitamin C.

How to Use

  1. Cleanse your face thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. If using a toner, apply it before the serum.
  3. Dispense 3-4 drops of the Radiance Serum onto your fingertips.
  4. Gently pat and press the serum into your skin, avoiding the eye area.
  5. Allow the serum to fully absorb (approximately 1-2 minutes).
  6. Follow with your favorite moisturizer.
  7. For daytime use, always finish with a broad-spectrum sunscreen.

Recommended use: Apply morning and evening for best results. If you have sensitive skin, start with once-daily application and gradually increase frequency as tolerated.

Note: A slight tingling sensation is normal when first using this product due to the active Vitamin C. If irritation persists, discontinue use and consult a dermatologist.
