Product Details

Our Hydrating Conditioner is a premium haircare product designed to provide intense moisture and repair to dry, damaged hair. The rich, creamy formula penetrates deep into the hair shaft to restore moisture from within, while also coating the hair cuticle to seal in hydration and prevent future damage.

This versatile conditioner can be used as a daily conditioner or as a weekly intensive treatment mask for extra hydration and repair. Despite its rich formula, it rinses clean without leaving residue or weighing hair down, making it suitable for all hair types from fine to coarse.

Each bottle contains 250ml (8.4 fl oz) of product and features a convenient flip-top cap for easy dispensing. The formula is color-safe and free from sulfates, parabens, and silicones, making it ideal for color-treated, chemically processed, or naturally dry hair.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Cetearyl Alcohol, Behentrimonium Chloride, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil, Hydrolyzed Silk Protein, Panthenol, Glycerin, Argania Spinosa Kernel Oil, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Citric Acid, Tetrasodium EDTA, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Fragrance.

Key Ingredients:

  • Shea Butter (Butyrospermum Parkii Butter): Rich in vitamins A, E, and F, shea butter provides deep moisture and helps repair the hair cuticle, reducing frizz and adding shine.
  • Coconut Oil (Cocos Nucifera Oil): Penetrates the hair shaft to provide deep hydration, prevent protein loss, and strengthen hair from within.
  • Hydrolyzed Silk Protein: Forms a protective barrier around the hair shaft, improving elasticity, adding shine, and enhancing manageability.
  • Argan Oil (Argania Spinosa Kernel Oil): Rich in antioxidants and essential fatty acids, argan oil helps nourish, moisturize, and protect hair from damage.
  • Aloe Vera (Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice): Soothes the scalp, balances pH levels, and adds lightweight moisture to hair.
  • Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5): Penetrates the hair shaft to moisturize from within, adding volume and shine while reducing frizz and split ends.

How to Use

As a Daily Conditioner:

  1. After shampooing, squeeze excess water from hair.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount to mid-lengths and ends (use more for longer or thicker hair).
  3. Gently comb through with fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute evenly.
  4. Leave on for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Rinse thoroughly with cool water to seal the cuticle and enhance shine.

As a Deep Treatment Mask:

  1. After shampooing, towel-dry hair to remove excess water.
  2. Apply a generous amount throughout hair, focusing on mid-lengths and ends.
  3. Comb through to ensure even distribution.
  4. Cover hair with a shower cap or warm towel to enhance penetration.
  5. Leave on for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Pro tip: For extremely dry or damaged hair, apply to dry hair before shampooing, leave on for 30 minutes, then shampoo and condition as usual for an extra boost of hydration.
