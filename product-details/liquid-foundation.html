Product Details

Our Liquid Foundation is a premium makeup product designed to provide flawless coverage while caring for your skin. The revolutionary formula adapts to your skin tone and texture, creating a natural-looking finish that lasts all day.

Available in 40 inclusive shades, this foundation is suitable for all skin tones and undertones. The buildable formula allows you to customize your coverage from medium to full, depending on your preference and needs.

Each bottle contains 30ml (1 fl oz) of product and features a convenient pump dispenser for easy, mess-free application. The formula is dermatologist-tested, non-comedogenic, and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Cyclopentasiloxane, Dimethicone, Glycerin, PEG-10 Dimethicone, Butylene Glycol, Phenyl Trimethicone, Sodium Chloride, Tocopheryl Acetate, Sodium Hyaluronate, Dimethicone/PEG-10/15 Crosspolymer, Nylon-12, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Hexylene Glycol.

Key Ingredients:

  • Hyaluronic Acid (Sodium Hyaluronate): Provides deep hydration and helps plump the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines.
  • Vitamin E (Tocopheryl Acetate): A powerful antioxidant that protects the skin from environmental damage and nourishes the skin.
  • Glycerin: A humectant that attracts and retains moisture in the skin, keeping it hydrated throughout the day.
  • Dimethicone: Creates a smooth, silky texture and helps the foundation glide effortlessly over the skin.

How to Use

  1. Start with clean, moisturized skin. Allow your moisturizer to fully absorb before applying foundation.
  2. If using a primer, apply it now and allow it to set for 30 seconds.
  3. Dispense 1-2 pumps of foundation onto the back of your hand.
  4. Using a foundation brush, makeup sponge, or fingertips, apply the foundation to the center of your face.
  5. Blend outward using gentle, sweeping or stippling motions.
  6. For additional coverage, apply a second layer to areas that need more coverage.
  7. Set with powder if desired for a longer-lasting finish.

Pro tip: For a more natural look, use less product and focus on areas that need coverage. For a more full-coverage look, apply a second layer all over the face.

Shade selection: If you're between shades, we recommend choosing the lighter shade for a more natural look.
