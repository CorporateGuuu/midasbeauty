Product Details

Our Hydrating Moisturizer is a premium skincare treatment designed to provide intense, long-lasting hydration while strengthening your skin's natural barrier. This luxurious formula combines ceramides, peptides, and natural oils to lock in moisture for up to 72 hours.

The non-greasy, fast-absorbing texture makes it perfect for all skin types, including sensitive and combination skin. It can be used both morning and night as part of your daily skincare routine.

Each jar contains 50ml (1.7 fl oz) of product and is packaged in a premium glass jar with an airless pump design to preserve the formula's potency and prevent contamination.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Glycerin, Cetearyl Alcohol, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Ceramide NP, Ceramide AP, Ceramide EOP, Phytosphingosine, Cholesterol, Sodium Hyaluronate, Niacinamide, Tocopherol, Panthenol, Sodium Lauroyl Lactylate, Xanthan Gum, Carbomer, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin.

Key Ingredients:

  • Ceramides: Essential lipids that help restore and maintain the skin's barrier, preventing moisture loss.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Attracts and retains up to 1000 times its weight in water, providing deep hydration.
  • Niacinamide: Improves skin texture, reduces pore size, and enhances barrier function.
  • Vitamin E (Tocopherol): A powerful antioxidant that protects against environmental damage.
  • Panthenol: Provides soothing hydration and promotes skin healing.

How to Use

  1. Cleanse your face thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. If using serums or treatments, apply those first.
  3. Take a small amount (about the size of a pea) of the Hydrating Moisturizer.
  4. Warm the product between your fingertips.
  5. Gently massage into your face and neck using upward, circular motions.
  6. Allow the moisturizer to fully absorb (approximately 1-2 minutes).
  7. For daytime use, follow with a broad-spectrum sunscreen.

Recommended use: Apply morning and evening for best results.

Pro tip: For extra hydration, apply to slightly damp skin to lock in additional moisture.
