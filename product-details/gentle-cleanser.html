Product Details

Our Gentle Cleanser is a sulfate-free facial cleanser designed to effectively remove makeup, dirt, and impurities without disrupting your skin's natural pH balance or stripping away essential moisture.

This gentle formula is enriched with soothing botanical extracts including aloe vera, chamomile, and cucumber to calm and nourish the skin while cleansing. The non-foaming, creamy texture provides a luxurious cleansing experience that leaves skin feeling soft, clean, and comfortable - never tight or dry.

Each bottle contains 200ml (6.7 fl oz) of product and features a convenient pump dispenser for easy application. The formula is fragrance-free, making it ideal for sensitive skin types and those with sensitivities to fragranced products.

Full Ingredients List

Water, Glycerin, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Sodium Lauroamphoacetate, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Cucumis Sativus (Cucumber) Fruit Extract, Panthenol, Allantoin, Sodium Hyaluronate, Citric Acid, Disodium EDTA, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin.

Key Ingredients:

  • Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate & Sodium Lauroamphoacetate: Mild, sulfate-free cleansing agents that effectively remove impurities without stripping the skin.
  • Aloe Vera: Soothes and calms irritated skin while providing light hydration.
  • Chamomile Extract: Contains anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce redness and irritation.
  • Cucumber Extract: Refreshes and cools the skin while providing antioxidant benefits.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Helps maintain skin hydration during the cleansing process.
  • Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5): Moisturizes and promotes skin healing.

How to Use

  1. Dispense 1-2 pumps of cleanser into your palm.
  2. Add a small amount of lukewarm water and work into a light lather between your hands.
  3. Gently massage onto damp face and neck using circular motions, avoiding the eye area.
  4. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Pat skin dry with a clean towel.
  6. Follow with your favorite toner, serum, and moisturizer.

Recommended use: Morning and evening as the first step in your skincare routine.

For makeup removal: For heavy makeup, consider double cleansing. First use an oil-based cleanser or micellar water to remove makeup, then follow with the Gentle Cleanser for a thorough cleanse.
